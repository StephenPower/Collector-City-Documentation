Store Overview

After You sign up for a store, you will recieve your user name and password. The default address of your store is xxxxxxxxxxx.example.com. The xxx's represent the store name you choose during setup. In this example, I choose yyzfan12345. On the upper left your store name is displayed. On the upper right the links, to help, getback to your store, about us, contact, and logout
