Let's Start with the Basics, the overlooked Blog...

There are 100s of millions of blogs, why do I need one? It's simple, people get more traffic with a blog:

 

  • It establishes your credibility
  • Provides industry / product knowledge & insight
  • Provides a reason for return visitors
  • Increases Sales

Some Hints

  • Post Reguarly, it's a amzing what once a week will do
  • Post On Topics that relate to your industry / shop / products
  • Drive Inbound Links with other industry information blogs
  • Provide Google GOOD CONTENT. Sorry for the screaming. Google is getting better and better at picking up the link farms and content farms.
  • It's your first step to social media, the world will see it and read, make it something you are proud of
  • Keywords and content related to the topic and what you are selling
