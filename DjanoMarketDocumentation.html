Django Market Documentation

Overview

Django Market Has Three Goals:

  1. Support Online Activities For Small to Medium Businesses:Marketing, Selling, Inventory Management & Customer Management
  2. Aggregate "normal" business activities (inventory, customers,auctions, selling) to trigger marketing, selling, searching activites. Same for blogging.
  3. SAAS Allow a single platform to host multiple marketplaces and multiple stores on each marketplace

Philosophy

  1. I'm not looking to build every conceivable feature.
  2. Focus on the features small business need and make sure they are intuitive and easy to use.
  3. Let's figure out what needs to be cut, that's just as important

Target Audience

  1. Small Business Owners Paying high listing fees at the usual places
  2. Designers, allowing them to build beautiful websites easily

Documentation Sets

There are Three Sets of Documentation

  1. Small Business Owners on how to use their shops
  2. Designers On How to Build shops
  3. Developers on how to install, run and extend Django Market

Extensive documentation is a sign of a poorly implemented software. No documentation is just lazy
